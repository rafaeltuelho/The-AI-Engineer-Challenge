## More Examples

### Inline Math
The quadratic formula is $x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$.

### Display Math
$$
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} dx = \sqrt{\pi}
$$

### Complex Expressions
$$
\begin{align}
\frac{\partial f}{\partial x} &= \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{f(x+h) - f(x)}{h} \\
\frac{\partial f}{\partial y} &= \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{f(y+h) - f(y)}{h}
\end{align}
$$

### Matrix

\end{pmatrix}

x \\

\end{pmatrix}
=
\begin{pmatrix}
ax + by \\
cx + dy
\end{pmatrix}
$$
